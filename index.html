What makes SmartLearn different

Designed for students and small institutes who want structure, clarity, and guidance.

Personal learning paths

Move through topics step by step with a visual path map that tracks what is not started, in progress, and mastered.

Smart study planner

Turn your exam date and available hours into a weekly study plan so you always know what to study next.

Skill-gap analytics

Simple dashboards highlight weak topics and recommend what to revise before your next quiz.

Mentor & doubt space

A dedicated space where learners can post questions and mentors can respond, keeping discussions organised.

Gamified learning

Micro-goals, streaks, and badges keep learners motivated to show up every day and complete small tasks.

Focus mode

A high-contrast, distraction-free theme designed for long study sessions and better readability.

How SmartLearn fits your semester

1

Set your goal

Choose your courses, exam date, and weekly study time. SmartLearn creates your first learning path.

2

Study and attempt

Follow the suggested modules, attempt quizzes, and record notes while you learn.

3

Review and improve

Use the analytics dashboard to find weak areas, plan revision, and unlock badges as you progress.
